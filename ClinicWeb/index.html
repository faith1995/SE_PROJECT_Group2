About AvoHealth Dental Clinic

At The AvoHealth Dental Clinic, we don’t underestimate the value of a great smile. We all know that a beautiful smile begins with healthy teeth. Each patient will receive outstanding, high quality, personalised specialist dental care in a relaxed and family friendly environment. To ensure that each patient’s unique needs are treated, we take time in our screenings to assess the most pressing concerns. A tailor-made treatment plan is then presented to the patient to determine the way forward.
